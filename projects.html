From the Big Bang to the Present Universe

Website Status: Under Active Development

Latest Developments: [22/03/2026]

This entire website stands as my most ambitious and deeply personal endeavor. It is a continuous labor of love, developed meticulously in the limited time available between my professional career commitments, extensive piano and composition practice, guitar studies, competitive gaming (Tekken 8), and my ongoing academic pursuits in C++, Python, SAP Fiori, SAP ERP, and many other intricate systems.

Projects like "From the Big Bang to the Present Universe" are just the beginning. This site is conceived as a lifelong undertaking, a project I intend to nurture and expand until the day I die. My ultimate vision is to cultivate it into one of the most comprehensive and authoritative digital resources on an ever-growing array of topics, including cosmology, quantum mechanics, quantum computing, astrophysics, general relativity, quantum field theory, fluid mechanics, black hole thermodynamics, string theory, M-theory, loop quantum gravity, dark matter, dark energy, among many other topics. Every project will include multiple individual pages for a deeper explanation or understanding of certain topics, such as Radiation, The Minds Behind the Big Bang, Quantum Entanglement, Schrödinger Equation, etc. These clickable individual pages are intentionally designed without overt indicators, inviting readers to discover them on their own, guided by their innate curiosity. The ultimate purpose of this site is to educate and inspire, helping people understand as many topics as possible that shed light on the universe, our existence, time, and beyond. I am taking very strict care of this site, making sure it is as detailed and accurate as possible. It will feature intricate 3D models and interactive visualizations to enhance understanding of complex concepts across these fields, with many more projects and topics to be added as time progresses and my capacity allows.

This platform is made publicly available in its current state and will continue to be as it progresses. It serves as a transparent showcase of my evolving knowledge in both the scientific domains it covers and my continuous journey in web development and coding. As such, it openly displays all stages of learning, including any nascent errors, which are systematically refined and eliminated as my skills in programming, web design, and theoretical physics continue to advance.

My sincere apologies in advance for any unfinished topics, incomplete explanations, or ongoing editions you may encounter. This continuous work is a direct result of balancing this passion project with my professional career commitments and other significant interests like music, gaming, and further academic studies, which means content is constantly being worked on, edited, and corrected as time allows.

For a better experience, please use a computer. Mobile user experience is still under development, and mobile devices may encounter glitches or unexpected behavior.
