Purpose of This Site

Last updated: May 24, 2026.

This website is my personal project for science education, interactive models, programming practice, music, Tekken, and my own interpretive work on time, infinity, quantum theory, and consciousness-first ontology. It is not a university publication, a medical service, a legal service, a financial service, or a professional consulting site.

The simplest way to read the site is by purpose: personal life, music, systems work, practice worlds, science models, and my interpretive work on time, infinity, and consciousness.

I try to separate established physics, active research, and personal metaphysical commitment. Scientific sections should be read as educational material. Personal sections should be read as my own writing and worldview, not as scientific consensus.

Site Version

This is a rebuilt version of a larger website project that has been forming since I was 25. Earlier versions helped me learn what the project needed, but this version uses a cleaner structure so the site can keep improving without mixing unrelated content or breaking sections unnecessarily.

Scientific and Educational Disclaimer

The scientific material is written for learning and interpretation. I use sources, equations, and references, but errors can still happen. Do not rely on this site as the only source for academic work, engineering decisions, medical decisions, legal decisions, or financial decisions. Verify major claims against primary sources, textbooks, professors, or qualified professionals.

Interactive simulations and visual models are educational approximations. They are designed to make ideas easier to understand, not to replace exact numerical solvers, laboratory instruments, or professional scientific software.

Personal and Metaphysical Disclaimer

My writing on Everettian reality, unified duality, finite configurations, ultimate totality, and consciousness-first reality is interpretive. Physics motivates parts of the framework, but it does not prove the full metaphysical view.

When I discuss consciousness, identity, death, meaning, or the total structure of reality, I am not presenting a completed physical theory. I am making a disciplined personal argument and marking the boundary between physics-facing support and personal ontology.

Privacy and Visitor Data

This site does not currently use a visitor counter, advertising tracker, login system, contact form, or third-party tracking cookie. The site stores only local browser preferences such as language, reading settings, and media choices when your browser allows local storage.

The site may load third-party resources such as fonts, icons, math rendering scripts, or embedded media. Those providers may receive ordinary request data from your browser. If I add analytics later, I will use a privacy-focused option where possible and update this notice before treating the counter as part of the site.

Security Notes

This is a static website with no user accounts and no database. That reduces the amount of sensitive information the site can collect or expose. The site also uses HTTPS, a Content Security Policy, noopener/noreferrer on external links, and youtube-nocookie.com for embedded YouTube content where applicable.

No website can honestly promise perfect security. If you notice a broken link, suspicious behavior, reading or usability issue, or content error, use the listed contact/social links to report it.

Reading and Usability Statement

I want the site to be usable with keyboard navigation, screen readers, larger text, reduced motion, higher contrast, visible links, and a readable font mode. The Reading Settings panel is built so visitors can adjust the experience without changing the core identity of the site.

Reading usability is an ongoing process, not a single checkbox. If something blocks reading, navigation, contrast, motion comfort, or media understanding, it should be treated as something to fix.
