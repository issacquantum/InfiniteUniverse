Technical Terms Glossary

  • ▣Derivative: A local rate of change.
  • ▣Eigenvalue: A scale factor for a vector whose direction is preserved by a linear transformation.
  • ▣Differential equation: An equation involving a quantity and its rates of change.
  • ▣Tensor: A mathematical object whose components change consistently across coordinate systems.
