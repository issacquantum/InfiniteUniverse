Derivative Notation

Equation

$$\frac{df}{dx}$$

Literal Reading

The derivative of \(f\) with respect to \(x\).

Mathematical Reading

It measures the local rate at which a function changes as its input changes.

Symbol Breakdown

\(f\): the function or quantity being measured.

\(x\): the input variable.

\(d/dx\): the operation of differentiating with respect to \(x\).

Meaning

Derivatives are the language of velocity, acceleration, growth, decay, slope, sensitivity, and local change.
