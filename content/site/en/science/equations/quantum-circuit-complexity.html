Quantum Circuit Complexity

Equation

$$ C_{\epsilon}(U)=\min\left\{m:\left\|U-G_m\cdots G_2G_1\right\|\le\epsilon\right\} $$

Literal Reading

C of vertical bar psi > equals minimum number of gates preparing vertical bar psi >.

Common Reading

Circuit complexity is the shortest gate recipe for building a target operation to a chosen accuracy.

Symbols

\(U\): the target unitary operation.

\(G_1,\ldots,G_m\): elementary gates from an allowed gate set.

\(\epsilon\): tolerated approximation error.

\(\|\cdot\|\): a norm measuring distance between operators.

Meaning of the Equation

It counts the smallest circuit needed to prepare a state.
