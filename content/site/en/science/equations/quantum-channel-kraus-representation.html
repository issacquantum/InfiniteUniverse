Quantum Channel Kraus Representation

Equation

$$ \mathcal{E}(\rho) = \sum_k K_k \rho K_k^\dagger,\qquad \sum_k K_k^\dagger K_k = I $$

Literal Reading

Read it as:  open parenthesis rho close parenthesis equals sum sub i K sub i rho K sub i dagger.

Common Reading

The general form of a quantum channel acting on a density matrix while preserving total probability.

Symbols

\(\mathcal{E}\): the quantum channel or physical process.

\(\rho\): the input density matrix.

\(K_k\): Kraus operators representing possible effects of the channel.

\(K_k^\dagger\): the conjugate transpose of \(K_k\).

\(I\): the identity operator.

Meaning of the Equation

A noisy quantum process is represented by operators acting on the density matrix.
