Geodesic Equation

Equation

$$\frac{d^2x^\mu}{d\tau^2} + \Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\frac{dx^\alpha}{d\tau}\frac{dx^\beta}{d\tau} = 0$$

Literal Reading

d to the power of 2x to the power of mu over dτ to the power of 2 plus Γ to the power of mu sub alpha beta of dx to the power of alpha over dτ of dx to the power of beta over dτ equals 0.

Common Reading

The equation for free fall motion through curved spacetime.

Symbols

\(x^\mu\): spacetime coordinates along the path.

\(\tau\): proper time along the object's worldline.

\(\Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\): Christoffel symbols, encoding how coordinates curve and change.

\(\alpha,\beta,\mu\): spacetime indices.

Meaning of the Equation

Free particles follow the straightest possible paths in curved spacetime.
