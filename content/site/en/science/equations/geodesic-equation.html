Geodesic Equation

Equation

$$\frac{d^2x^\mu}{d\tau^2} + \Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\frac{dx^\alpha}{d\tau}\frac{dx^\beta}{d\tau} = 0$$

Literal Reading

The second derivative of x mu with respect to tau plus Gamma mu alpha beta times the velocity components equals zero.

Common Reading

Short version: The equation for free fall motion through curved spacetime.

Symbols

\(x^\mu\): spacetime coordinates along the path.

\(\tau\): proper time along the object's worldline.

\(\Gamma^\mu_{\alpha\beta}\): Christoffel symbols, encoding how coordinates curve and change.

\(\alpha,\beta,\mu\): spacetime indices.

Meaning of the Equation

The equation for free fall motion through curved spacetime.
