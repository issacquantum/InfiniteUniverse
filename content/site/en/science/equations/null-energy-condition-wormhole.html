Null Energy Condition

Equation

$$ T_{\mu\nu}k^\mu k^\nu < 0 $$

Literal Reading

T sub open brace mu nu close brace k to the power of mu k to the power of nu greater than or equal to 0.

Common Reading

For a traversable wormhole throat, the required stress energy can violate the usual null energy condition.

Symbols

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

\(k^\mu\): null vector, meaning a lightlike direction.

\(<0\): negative value for this contraction.

Meaning of the Equation

Ordinary classical matter usually satisfies this condition. Violating it signals the exotic stress-energy problem behind traversable wormhole proposals.
