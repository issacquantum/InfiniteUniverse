Null Energy Condition

Equation

$$ T_{\mu\nu}k^\mu k^\nu < 0 $$

Literal Reading

T sub open brace mu nu close brace k to the power of mu k to the power of nu greater than or equal to 0.

Common Reading

The null energy condition requires \(T_{\mu u}k^\mu k^ u\ge0\) for all null vectors \(k^\mu\).

Symbols

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

\(k^\mu\): null vector, meaning a lightlike direction.

\(<0\): negative value for this contraction.

Meaning of the Equation

Traversable Morris-Thorne wormholes require NEC violation near the throat.
