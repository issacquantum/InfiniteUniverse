Null Energy Condition

Equation

$$ T_{\mu\nu}k^\mu k^\nu < 0 $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the stress energy tensor contracted twice with a null vector is less than zero.

Common Reading

For a traversable wormhole throat, the required stress energy can violate the usual null energy condition.

Symbols

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

\(k^\mu\): null vector, meaning a lightlike direction.

\(<0\): negative value for this contraction.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects geometry, motion, horizons, or gravitational sources. It tells what clocks, distances, curvature, or observers would measure, so the physical claim is tied to spacetime structure rather than to a picture or metaphor.
