Null Energy Condition

Equation

$$ T_{\mu\nu}k^\mu k^\nu < 0 $$

What the Formula Says

The stress energy tensor contracted twice with a null vector is less than zero.

What It Means in Practice

For a traversable wormhole throat, the required stress energy can violate the usual null energy condition.

Symbols

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

\(k^\mu\): null vector, meaning a lightlike direction.

\(<0\): negative value for this contraction.

Why It Matters

This is why traversable wormholes are not ordinary tunnels. They require matter or quantum effects that do not behave like normal positive energy matter.
