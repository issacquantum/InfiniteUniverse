Transformer Residual Block

Equation

$$ y=\operatorname{LayerNorm}(x+\operatorname{Sublayer}(x)) $$

Literal Reading

x sub l plus 1 equals x sub l plus F of x sub l.

Common Reading

The residual path lets information and gradients move through deep transformer stacks without forcing every layer to relearn the identity map.

Symbols

\(x\): incoming representation.

\(\operatorname{Sublayer}(x)\): attention or feed-forward transformation.

\(\operatorname{LayerNorm}\): normalization that stabilizes activations.

Meaning of the Equation

It connects the quantities in the formula so the relation can be used to compute or interpret a situation in Artificial Intelligence.
