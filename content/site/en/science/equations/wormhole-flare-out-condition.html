Wormhole Flare-Out Condition

Equation

$$ b(r_0)=r_0,\qquad b'(r_0)<1 $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: b' open parenthesis r0 close parenthesis < 1.

Common Reading

This says the spatial geometry opens outward at the throat instead of pinching shut.

Symbols

\(b(r)\): shape function controlling the wormhole's radial geometry.

\(r_0\): throat radius, the narrowest allowed radius.

\(b'(r_0)\): slope of the shape function at the throat.

Meaning of the Equation

These conditions describe a throat geometry and whether it can avoid horizons.
