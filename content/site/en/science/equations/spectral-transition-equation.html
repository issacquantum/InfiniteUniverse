Spectral Transition Equation

Equation

$$ \Delta E = h\nu = E_i - E_f $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: delta E equals h nu equals E sub i minus E sub f.

Common Reading

The emitted or absorbed photon energy equals the difference between two allowed energy levels.

Symbols

\(\Delta E\): the energy difference between two allowed states.

\(h\nu\): the photon energy.

\(E_i\): the initial energy level.

\(E_f\): the final energy level.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects microscopic structure, energy, probability, or reaction behavior to a measurable physical result. It shows which conditions control the process and what changes when temperature, energy, particles, or available states change.
