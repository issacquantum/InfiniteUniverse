Spectral Transition Equation

Equation

$$ \Delta E = h\nu = E_i - E_f $$

What the Formula Says

Delta E equals h nu equals E sub i minus E sub f.

What It Means in Practice

This means the emitted or absorbed photon energy equals the difference between two allowed energy levels.

Symbols

\(\Delta E\): the energy difference between two allowed states.

\(h\nu\): the photon energy.

\(E_i\): the initial energy level.

\(E_f\): the final energy level.

Equation Meaning

Atoms emit or absorb radiation only when they jump between discrete energy levels. The photon carries exactly that energy gap.
