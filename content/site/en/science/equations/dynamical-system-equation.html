Dynamical System Equation

Equation

$$\frac{d\mathbf{x}}{dt}=\mathbf{F}(\mathbf{x})$$

Literal Reading

The rate of change of the state vector equals a function of the state.

Systems Reading

The present state determines how the system begins to move next. Nonlinear choices of \(\mathbf{F}\) can create cycles, attractors, or chaos.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\mathbf{x}\): state vector.

\(t\): time.

\(\mathbf{F}\): rule that assigns a change direction to each state.

Meaning

This compact form appears across mechanics, biology, networks, climate models, control systems, and many simulations.
