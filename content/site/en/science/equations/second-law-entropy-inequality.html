Second Law Entropy Inequality

Equation

$$dS\geq \frac{\delta Q}{T}$$

Plain Meaning

In an irreversible process, entropy increases by more than the reversible heat-flow part alone.

Symbols

  • ▣\(dS\): entropy change.
  • ▣\(\delta Q\): heat transferred.
  • ▣\(T\): absolute temperature.

How to Read It

Equality is the ideal reversible limit. The greater-than part marks irreversibility and entropy production.

What It Does Not Say

It does not say small fluctuations are impossible. It is a macroscopic thermodynamic statement.
