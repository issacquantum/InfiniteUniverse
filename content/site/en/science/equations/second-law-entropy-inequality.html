Second Law Entropy Inequality

Equation

$$dS\geq \frac{\delta Q}{T}$$

Literal Reading

Delta S sub universe greater than or equal to 0.

Common Reading

Entropy measures uncertainty, disorder, or missing information in the relevant physical or informational state.

Symbols

  • \(dS\): entropy change.
  • \(\delta Q\): heat transferred.
  • \(T\): absolute temperature.

Meaning of the Equation

Entropy increase reflects the overwhelming number of microscopic arrangements corresponding to higher-entropy macrostates, given a low-entropy past condition.
