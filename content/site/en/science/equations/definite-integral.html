Definite Integral

Equation

$$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx$$

Plain Meaning

This adds up many tiny contributions of \(f(x)\) from \(a\) to \(b\).

Symbols

  • ▣\(a,b\): the starting and ending limits.
  • ▣\(f(x)\): the quantity being accumulated.
  • ▣\(dx\): a tiny piece of the input axis.

How to Read It

Read it as total accumulation. Area under a curve is the visual example, but the same idea gives work, probability, mass, charge, and action.

What It Does Not Say

It does not always mean literal geometric area. The meaning depends on what \(f(x)\) represents.
