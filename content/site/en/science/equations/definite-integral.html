Definite Integral

Equation

$$\int_a^b f(x)\,dx$$

Literal Reading

The integral of \(f(x)\) from \(a\) to \(b\).

Mathematical Reading

It measures accumulated quantity over an interval, often represented as area under a curve.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\int\): accumulation operation.

\(a,b\): lower and upper limits.

\(f(x)\): the quantity being accumulated.

\(dx\): infinitesimal input element.

Meaning

Integrals describe accumulated distance, total mass, probability, work, charge, energy, and many other physical totals.
