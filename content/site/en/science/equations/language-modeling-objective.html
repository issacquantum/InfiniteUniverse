Language Modeling Objective

Equation

$$ L_{\rm LM}=-\sum_t \log p_\theta(x_t\mid x_{1:t-1}) $$

Literal Reading

The loss is the negative sum of the log probability assigned to each next token given the previous tokens.

Physicist's Reading

The model is trained to predict the next token from context. Repeating that objective at scale produces broad language and task behavior.

Symbol Breakdown

\(x_t\): token at position \(t\).

\(x_{1:t-1}\): previous context tokens.

\(p_\theta\): probability distribution produced by parameters \(\theta\).

Physical Meaning

The objective optimizes prediction, not truth or wisdom. Useful behavior emerges only when prediction is paired with data quality, evaluation, and alignment.
