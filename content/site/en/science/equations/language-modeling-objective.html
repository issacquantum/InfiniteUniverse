Language Modeling Objective

Equation

$$ L_{\rm LM}=-\sum_t \log p_\theta(x_t\mid x_{1:t-1}) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the loss is the negative sum of the log probability assigned to each next token given the previous tokens.

Common Reading

The model is trained to predict the next token from context.

Symbols

\(x_t\): token at position \(t\).

\(x_{1:t-1}\): previous context tokens.

\(p_\theta\): probability distribution produced by parameters \(\theta\).

Meaning of the Equation

This relation turns an algorithmic step into a precise rule. It identifies what is being updated, counted, or bounded, so the process can be checked by its state, cost, error, or learning behavior instead of only by a visual animation.
