Language Modeling Objective

Equation

$$ L_{\rm LM}=-\sum_t \log p_\theta(x_t\mid x_{1:t-1}) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: max sum sub t log P open parenthesis x sub t vertical bar x sub < t close parenthesis.

Common Reading

The model is trained to predict the next token from context.

Symbols

\(x_t\): token at position \(t\).

\(x_{1:t-1}\): previous context tokens.

\(p_\theta\): probability distribution produced by parameters \(\theta\).

Meaning of the Equation

The model is trained to assign high probability to the next token.
