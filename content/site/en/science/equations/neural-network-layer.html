Neural Network Layer

Equation

$$ h=\sigma(Wx+b) $$

What the Formula Says

h equals sigma applied to W times x plus b.

What It Means in Practice

This is a learned transformation followed by a nonlinear response, allowing the model to represent patterns beyond a single linear map.

Symbols

\(x\): input vector.

\(W\): learned weight matrix.

\(b\): learned bias vector.

\(\sigma\): nonlinear activation function.

\(h\): output representation of the layer.

Equation Meaning

The equation is not biological realism. It is a compact computational rule for transforming input structure into a representation that can be trained.
