Time-Independent Schrödinger Equation

Equation

$$ \hat{H}\psi(\mathbf{r}) = E\psi(\mathbf{r}) $$

Literal Reading

H psi equals E psi.

Common Reading

The quantum state is governed by the Hamiltonian, which determines its time evolution or allowed energies.

Symbols

\(\hat{H}\): the Hamiltonian operator.

\(\psi(\mathbf{r})\): a stationary spatial wavefunction.

\(E\): the allowed energy eigenvalue.

\(\mathbf{r}\): the spatial position vector.

Meaning of the Equation

Stationary states are energy eigenstates of the Hamiltonian. Their spatial shape is fixed while their time dependence is only a phase.
