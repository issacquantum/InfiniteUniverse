Gibbs Free Energy

Equation

$$\Delta G=\Delta H-T\Delta S$$

Plain Meaning

This estimates whether a process is thermodynamically favored at constant temperature and pressure.

Symbols

  • ▣\(\Delta G\): Gibbs free-energy change.
  • ▣\(\Delta H\): enthalpy change.
  • ▣\(T\): absolute temperature.
  • ▣\(\Delta S\): entropy change.

How to Read It

Negative \(\Delta G\) means favored under those conditions. Positive means not favored without added work or coupling.

What It Does Not Say

It does not tell reaction speed. Kinetics and activation barriers decide how fast it happens.
