Atomic Recombination

Equation

$$\text{nuclei} + \text{electrons} \rightarrow \text{atoms}$$

Literal Reading

p plus e to the power of minus goes to H plus gamma.

Common Reading

Nuclei and electrons combine to form neutral atoms.

Symbols

nuclei: atomic nuclei.

electrons: negatively charged leptons.

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

Meaning of the Equation

It summarizes a key reaction in the formation of nuclei, atoms, molecules, or stellar energy.
