Atomic Recombination

Equation

$$\text{nuclei} + \text{electrons} \rightarrow \text{atoms}$$

Literal Reading

Nuclei plus electrons go to atoms.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist would read it as nuclei and electrons combine to form neutral atoms.

Symbol Breakdown

nuclei: atomic nuclei.

electrons: negatively charged leptons.

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

Physical Meaning

This summarizes recombination, the epoch when the universe cooled enough for electrons to bind to nuclei. Once neutral atoms formed, photons could travel freely, giving rise to the cosmic microwave background.
