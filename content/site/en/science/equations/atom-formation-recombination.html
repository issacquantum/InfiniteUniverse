Atomic Recombination

Equation

$$\text{nuclei} + \text{electrons} \rightarrow \text{atoms}$$

Literal Reading

p plus e to the power of minus goes to H plus gamma.

Common Reading

A proton and electron form neutral hydrogen while emitting a photon.

Symbols

nuclei: atomic nuclei.

electrons: negatively charged leptons.

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

Meaning of the Equation

In cosmology this marks recombination and last scattering, despite the historical name.
