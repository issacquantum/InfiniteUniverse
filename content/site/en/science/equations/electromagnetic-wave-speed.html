Electromagnetic Wave Speed

Equation

$$c=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0\epsilon_0}}$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: c equals one divided by the square root of mu naught times epsilon naught.

Common Reading

A short way to say it: In vacuum, the electric and magnetic constants combine to give the speed of electromagnetic waves.

Symbols

  • \(c\): speed of light in vacuum.
  • \(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.
  • \(\epsilon_0\): vacuum permittivity.

Meaning of the Equation

In vacuum, the electric and magnetic constants combine to give the speed of electromagnetic waves.
