Electromagnetic Wave Speed

Equation

$$c=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0\epsilon_0}}$$

Literal Reading

The wave speed is the inverse square root of vacuum permeability times vacuum permittivity.

Physicist's Reading

Maxwell's equations imply that electric and magnetic fields can sustain waves, and those waves move at the speed of light.

Symbol Breakdown

\(c\): speed of light in vacuum.

\(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.

\(\epsilon_0\): vacuum permittivity.

Physical Meaning

The equation revealed light as an electromagnetic wave and helped expose the need for special relativity.
