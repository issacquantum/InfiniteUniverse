Electromagnetic Wave Speed

Equation

$$c=\frac{1}{\sqrt{\mu_0\epsilon_0}}$$

Plain Meaning

In vacuum, the electric and magnetic constants combine to give the speed of electromagnetic waves.

Symbols

  • \(c\): speed of light in vacuum.
  • \(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.
  • \(\epsilon_0\): vacuum permittivity.

How to Read It

Read it as the Maxwell-equation prediction that light is an electromagnetic wave.

Meaning in Context

The equation gives the vacuum speed set by electric permittivity and magnetic permeability. In materials, the effective electromagnetic response changes the wave speed.
