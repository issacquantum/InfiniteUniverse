Hardy-Weinberg Equilibrium

Equation

$$p^2+2pq+q^2=1$$

Literal Reading

p to the power of 2 plus 2pq plus q to the power of 2 equals 1.

Common Reading

If assumptions are ideal, allele frequencies predict genotype frequencies in the next generation.

Symbols

  • \(p\): frequency of one allele.
  • \(q\): frequency of the other allele.
  • \(p^2,2pq,q^2\): genotype frequencies.

Meaning of the Equation

This is the baseline for a population with no evolutionary forces acting in the idealized model. When real data depart from it, the departure tells researchers which assumptions may have failed.
