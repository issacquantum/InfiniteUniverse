Hardy-Weinberg Equilibrium

Equation

$$p^2+2pq+q^2=1$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: p squared plus two p q plus q squared equals one.

Common Reading

If assumptions are ideal, allele frequencies predict genotype frequencies in the next generation.

Symbols

  • \(p\): frequency of one allele.
  • \(q\): frequency of the other allele.
  • \(p^2,2pq,q^2\): genotype frequencies.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects microscopic structure, energy, probability, or reaction behavior to a measurable physical result. It shows which conditions control the process and what changes when temperature, energy, particles, or available states change.
