RK4 (Fourth-Order Runge-Kutta) Global Error Order

Equation

$$ \|e_N\|=O(h^4) $$

What the Formula Says

The total error after many RK4 steps scales like the fourth power of the step size.

What It Means in Practice

For smooth problems, RK4 gains much more accuracy from a smaller step than Euler does.

Symbols

\(\|e_N\|\): final accumulated error.

\(O(h^4)\): fourth-order global scaling.

\(h\): numerical time step.

Why It Matters

RK4 is not magic, but it samples the slope enough times to follow smooth trajectories with much less drift.
