Euler Method

Equation

$$ y_{n+1}=y_n+h f(t_n,y_n) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: y sub open brace n plus 1 close brace equals y sub n plus h f open parenthesis t sub n comma y sub n close parenthesis.

Common Reading

This advances a system forward using a local derivative as an approximation to the next state.

Symbols

\(y_n\): current state.

\(y_{n+1}\): next state.

\(h\): step size.

\(f(t_n,y_n)\): derivative or slope at the current point.

Meaning of the Equation

Euler advances a differential equation by taking a straight-line step.
