Euler Method

Equation

$$ y_{n+1}=y_n+h f(t_n,y_n) $$

Literal Reading

The next value of y equals the current value plus the step size times the slope at the current point.

Physicist's Reading

This advances a system forward using a local derivative as an approximation to the next state.

Symbol Breakdown

\(y_n\): current state.

\(y_{n+1}\): next state.

\(h\): step size.

\(f(t_n,y_n)\): derivative or slope at the current point.

Physical Meaning

Euler's method is simple and transparent, but accuracy depends strongly on step size and stability.
