Mass-Energy Equivalence

Equation

$$E = mc^2$$

Literal Reading

E equals m c to the power of 2.

Common Reading

Rest mass corresponds to rest energy.

Symbols

\(E\): energy.

\(m\): mass.

\(c\): speed of light in vacuum.

Meaning of the Equation

Changes in invariant mass or binding energy can appear as kinetic energy or radiation.
