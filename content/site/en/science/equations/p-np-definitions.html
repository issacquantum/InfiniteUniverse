P and NP (nondeterministic polynomial time) Definitions

Equation

$$ P=\bigcup_{k\ge1}\mathrm{TIME}(n^k),\qquad NP=\bigcup_{k\ge1}\mathrm{NTIME}(n^k) $$

Literal Reading

P equals problems solvable in polynomial nomial time; NP equals problems verifiable in polynomial nomial time.

Common Reading

This separates finding a solution from checking one.

Symbols

\(\mathrm{TIME}(n^k)\): deterministic computation bounded by a polynomial.

\(\mathrm{NTIME}(n^k)\): nondeterministic polynomial-time computation.

\(\bigcup_{k\ge1}\): union over all polynomial degrees.

Meaning of the Equation

\(P\subseteq NP\), but whether \(P=NP\) is unresolved.
