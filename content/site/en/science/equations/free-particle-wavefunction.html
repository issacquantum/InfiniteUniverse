Free Particle Wavefunction

Equation

$$ \psi(x,t)=Ae^{i(kx-\omega t)} $$

Literal Reading

psi of x comma t equals A e to the i times k x minus omega t.

Common Reading

A time-dependent plane-wave solution for a free particle.

Symbols

\(\psi(x,t)\): the particle's wavefunction at position \(x\) and time \(t\).

\(A\): the amplitude of the wave.

\(k\): wave number, related to momentum.

\(\omega\): angular frequency, related to energy.

Meaning of the Equation

This is an ideal plane-wave momentum eigenstate and is not normalizable over all space unless treated with box normalization or wave packets.
