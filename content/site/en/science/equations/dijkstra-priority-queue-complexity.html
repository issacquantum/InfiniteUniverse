Dijkstra Priority Queue Complexity

Equation

$$ O((V+E)\log V) $$

Literal Reading

Big O of the quantity V plus E times log V.

Common Reading

Using a priority queue makes shortest-path exploration scale with graph size while paying a logarithmic cost for ordering candidates.

Symbols

\(V\): number of vertices or nodes.

\(E\): number of edges.

\(\log V\): cost of priority queue operations in common heap implementations.

Meaning of the Equation

Relaxation updates shortest-path estimates; priority queues control the runtime.
