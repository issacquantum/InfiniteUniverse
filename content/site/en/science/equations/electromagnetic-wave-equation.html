Electromagnetic Wave Equation

Equation

$$\nabla^2\mathbf{E}-\frac{1}{c^2}\frac{\partial^2\mathbf{E}}{\partial t^2}=0$$

Literal Reading

nabla to the power of 2 E minus of 1 over c to the power of 2 partial to the power of 2E over partial t to the power of 2 equals 0.

Common Reading

The electric field can propagate as a wave through empty space.

Symbols

  • \(\nabla^2\): spatial curvature of the field.
  • \(\partial^2/\partial t^2\): second time change.
  • \(c\): wave speed in vacuum.

Meaning of the Equation

The wave exists because changing electric and magnetic fields regenerate each other. This allows light to travel through vacuum without a material medium.
