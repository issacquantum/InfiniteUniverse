Electromagnetic Wave Equation

Equation

$$\nabla^2\mathbf{E}-\frac{1}{c^2}\frac{\partial^2\mathbf{E}}{\partial t^2}=0$$

Plain Meaning

The electric field can propagate as a wave through empty space.

Symbols

  • \(\nabla^2\): spatial curvature of the field.
  • \(\partial^2/\partial t^2\): second time change.
  • \(c\): wave speed in vacuum.

How to Read It

If the field bends in space, it changes in time in a way that carries the disturbance forward.

Meaning in Context

The equation describes ideal electromagnetic waves in vacuum. Media, sources, absorption, and quantum effects are added by extending the model.
