General Spacetime Interval

Equation

$$ds^2 = g_{\mu\nu}\,dx^\mu dx^\nu$$

Literal Reading

ds to the power of 2 equals g sub open brace mu nu close brace dx to the power of mu dx to the power of nu.

Common Reading

The spacetime interval combines time and space into one geometric separation.

Symbols

\(ds^2\): infinitesimal spacetime interval.

\(g_{\mu\nu}\): metric tensor of the spacetime geometry.

\(dx^\mu\) and \(dx^\nu\): infinitesimal coordinate separations.

Meaning of the Equation

The spacetime interval classifies separations and remains invariant between observers.
