QFT (quantum field theory) Microcausality

Equation

$$[\phi(x),\phi(y)] = 0\qquad \text{for spacelike separation}$$

Literal Reading

The commutator of field operators at x and y equals zero when the separation is spacelike.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist reads this as the local causality condition of relativistic quantum field theory.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\phi(x)\): field operator at spacetime point \(x\).

\([\ ,\ ]\): commutator.

Spacelike separation: events outside each other's light cones.

Physical Meaning

Microcausality prevents controllable faster than light influence in QFT while still allowing quantum correlations.
