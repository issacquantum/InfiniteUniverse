QFT (quantum field theory) Microcausality

Equation

$$[\phi(x),\phi(y)] = 0\qquad \text{for spacelike separation}$$

Literal Reading

open bracket phi of x comma phi of y close bracket equals 0 for spacelike separation.

Common Reading

The local causality condition of relativistic quantum field theory.

Symbols

\(\phi(x)\): field operator at spacetime point \(x\).

\([\ ,\ ]\): commutator.

Spacelike separation: events outside each other's light cones.

Meaning of the Equation

Spacelike separated operations cannot be used to send controlled messages outside the light cone. The commutator condition enforces that causal structure directly in the field operators.
