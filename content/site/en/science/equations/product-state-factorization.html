Product State Factorization

Equation

$$ |\phi\rangle=|\phi_1\rangle\otimes|\phi_2\rangle\otimes\cdots\otimes|\phi_n\rangle $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: vertical bar psi sub AB > equals vertical bar psi sub A > tensor vertical bar psi sub B >.

Common Reading

A product state is a state with no entanglement between the listed subsystems in the chosen factorization.

Symbols

\(|\phi\rangle\): full system state.

\(|\phi_i\rangle\): state of subsystem \(i\).

\(\otimes\): tensor product, used to combine subsystem Hilbert spaces.

\(\cdots\): continuation through all \(n\) subsystems.

Meaning of the Equation

Subsystems are unentangled when the joint state factorizes.
