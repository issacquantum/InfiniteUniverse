Runge Kutta Four

Equation

$$ y_{n+1}=y_n+\frac{h}{6}(k_1+2k_2+2k_3+k_4) $$

What the Formula Says

The next value equals the current value plus a weighted average of four slope estimates.

What It Means in Practice

RK4 (fourth-order Runge-Kutta) (Runge-Kutta fourth-order method) samples the derivative within a step to get a more accurate update than Euler's method.

Symbols

\(h\): step size.

\(k_1,k_2,k_3,k_4\): slope estimates inside the current step.

\(y_n\) and \(y_{n+1}\): current and next states.

Equation Meaning

RK4 is widely used because it balances accuracy and simplicity for many smooth ordinary differential equations.
