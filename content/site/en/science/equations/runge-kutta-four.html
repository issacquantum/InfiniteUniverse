Runge Kutta Four

Equation

$$ y_{n+1}=y_n+\frac{h}{6}(k_1+2k_2+2k_3+k_4) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: y sub open brace n plus 1 close brace equals y sub n plus h over 6 open parenthesis k1 plus 2k2 plus 2k3 plus k4 close parenthesis.

Common Reading

RK4 (fourth-order Runge-Kutta) (Runge-Kutta fourth-order method) samples the derivative within a step to get a more accurate update than Euler's method.

Symbols

\(h\): step size.

\(k_1,k_2,k_3,k_4\): slope estimates inside the current step.

\(y_n\) and \(y_{n+1}\): current and next states.

Meaning of the Equation

RK4 uses several slope estimates to get a much more accurate step.
