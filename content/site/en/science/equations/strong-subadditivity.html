Strong Subadditivity

Equation

$$ S(ABC) + S(B) \le S(AB) + S(BC) $$

Literal Reading

Common Reading

A deep constraint on how correlations can be distributed among three quantum systems.

Symbols

\(A\), \(B\), \(C\): three subsystems.

\(S(ABC)\): entropy of the combined system.

\(S(AB)\), \(S(BC)\): entropy of paired subsystems.

Meaning of the Equation

The entropies of overlapping quantum systems obey consistency constraints.
