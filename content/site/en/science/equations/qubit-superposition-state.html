Qubit Superposition State

Equation

$$ |\psi\rangle = \alpha|0\rangle + \beta|1\rangle $$

Literal Reading

Psi ket equals alpha times zero ket plus beta times one ket.

Common Reading

Short version: The most general pure state of a single qubit is a superposition of the computational basis states zero and one.

Symbols

\(|\psi\rangle\): the full quantum state of the qubit.

\(\alpha\): the complex amplitude attached to the basis state \(|0\rangle\).

\(\beta\): the complex amplitude attached to the basis state \(|1\rangle\).

\(|0\rangle\): the computational basis state zero.

\(|1\rangle\): the computational basis state one.

Meaning of the Equation

The most general pure state of a single qubit is a superposition of the computational basis states zero and one.
