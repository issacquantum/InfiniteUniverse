Lorentz Factor

Equation

$$\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - v^2/c^2}}$$

Literal Reading

gamma equals 1 over square root of 1 minus v to the power of 2 over c to the power of 2.

Common Reading

The scale factor that controls relativistic time dilation, length contraction, and energy growth.

Symbols

\(\gamma\): Lorentz factor.

\(v\): relative speed between inertial frames.

\(c\): speed of light in vacuum.

Meaning of the Equation

This factor measures how strong relativistic effects become as speed approaches light speed.
