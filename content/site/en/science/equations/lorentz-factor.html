Lorentz Factor

Equation

$$\gamma = \frac{1}{\sqrt{1 - v^2/c^2}}$$

Literal Reading

Gamma equals one divided by the square root of one minus v squared over c squared.

Common Reading

Short version: The scale factor that controls relativistic time dilation, length contraction, and energy growth.

Symbols

\(\gamma\): Lorentz factor.

\(v\): relative speed between inertial frames.

\(c\): speed of light in vacuum.

Meaning of the Equation

The scale factor that controls relativistic time dilation, length contraction, and energy growth.
