Quark-Antiquark Pair Production

Equation

$$\gamma + \gamma \rightarrow q + \bar{q}$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: gamma plus gamma goes to q plus q bar.

Common Reading

Two photons can produce a quark-antiquark pair if the available energy exceeds the threshold.

Symbols

\(\gamma\): photon.

\(q\): quark.

\(\bar{q}\): antiquark.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects microscopic structure, energy, probability, or reaction behavior to a measurable physical result. It shows which conditions control the process and what changes when temperature, energy, particles, or available states change.
