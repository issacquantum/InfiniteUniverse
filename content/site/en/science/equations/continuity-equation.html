Continuity Equation

Equation

$$ \frac{\partial |\Psi|^2}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot \mathbf{J} = 0 $$

Literal Reading

partial rho over partial t plus nabla dot j equals 0.

Common Reading

The time change of probability density plus the divergence of probability current equals zero.

Symbols

\(\partial |\Psi|^2 / \partial t\): the local rate of change of probability density.

\(\nabla \cdot \mathbf{J}\): the divergence of the probability current.

\(0\): the statement that probability is neither created nor destroyed locally.

Meaning of the Equation

Probability or density is locally conserved: change in a region comes from flow in or out.
