Feynman Propagator

Equation

$$D_F(x-y)=\langle 0|T\{\phi(x)\phi(y)\}|0\rangle$$

Literal Reading

Common Reading

The amplitude structure describing how field disturbances correlate between spacetime points.

Symbols

\(D_F\): Feynman propagator.

\(T\): time ordering operator.

\(|0\rangle\): vacuum state.

Meaning of the Equation

The propagator is the amplitude for field influence to travel between two points.
