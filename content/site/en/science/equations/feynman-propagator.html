Feynman Propagator

Equation

$$D_F(x-y)=\langle 0|T\{\phi(x)\phi(y)\}|0\rangle$$

Literal Reading

Delta sub F of x minus y equals < 0 vertical bar T phi of x phi of y vertical bar 0 >.

Common Reading

The amplitude structure describing how field disturbances correlate between spacetime points.

Symbols

\(D_F\): Feynman propagator.

\(T\): time ordering operator.

\(|0\rangle\): vacuum state.

Meaning of the Equation

The propagator is a building block for quantum field calculations. It encodes both how disturbances connect two points and the causal prescription used in amplitudes.
