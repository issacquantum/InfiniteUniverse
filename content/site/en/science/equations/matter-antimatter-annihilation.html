Matter-Antimatter Annihilation

Equation

$$\text{particle} + \text{antiparticle} \rightarrow \gamma + \gamma$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: particle plus antiparticle goes to gamma plus gamma.

Common Reading

A particle and its antiparticle annihilate into photons.

Symbols

particle: a matter particle.

antiparticle: the corresponding antimatter partner.

\(\gamma\): photon.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation states how the named quantities depend on each other. Its purpose is to turn a conceptual statement into a rule that can be calculated, compared, or tested inside the model where it is used.
