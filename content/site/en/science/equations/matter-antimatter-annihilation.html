Matter-Antimatter Annihilation

Equation

$$\text{particle} + \text{antiparticle} \rightarrow \gamma + \gamma$$

Literal Reading

e to the power of minus plus e to the power of plus goes to gamma plus gamma.

Common Reading

A particle and its antiparticle annihilate into photons.

Symbols

particle: a matter particle.

antiparticle: the corresponding antimatter partner.

\(\gamma\): photon.

Meaning of the Equation

Energy can create particle-antiparticle pairs, and pairs can annihilate back into radiation.
