Matter-Antimatter Annihilation

Equation

$$\text{particle} + \text{antiparticle} \rightarrow \gamma + \gamma$$

What the Formula Says

Particle plus antiparticle goes to gamma plus gamma.

What It Means in Practice

This means a particle and its antiparticle annihilate into photons.

Symbols

particle: a matter particle.

antiparticle: the corresponding antimatter partner.

\(\gamma\): photon.

Why It Matters

When a particle meets its antiparticle, their mass and kinetic energy can be converted into radiation. In the early universe, this happened constantly. The small leftover excess of matter is why visible matter exists at all today.
