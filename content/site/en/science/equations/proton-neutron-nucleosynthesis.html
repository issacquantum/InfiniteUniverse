Proton-Neutron Nucleosynthesis

Equation

$$\text{protons} + \text{neutrons} \rightarrow \text{atomic nuclei}$$

Literal Reading

p plus n goes to D plus gamma.

Common Reading

Protons and neutrons combine to form light atomic nuclei.

Symbols

protons: positively charged baryons.

neutrons: neutral baryons.

atomic nuclei: bound nuclear states such as deuterium or helium nuclei.

Meaning of the Equation

It summarizes a key reaction in the formation of nuclei, atoms, molecules, or stellar energy.
