Proton-Neutron Nucleosynthesis

Equation

$$\text{protons} + \text{neutrons} \rightarrow \text{atomic nuclei}$$

What the Formula Says

Protons plus neutrons go to atomic nuclei.

What It Means in Practice

This means protons and neutrons combine to form light atomic nuclei.

Symbols

protons: positively charged baryons.

neutrons: neutral baryons.

atomic nuclei: bound nuclear states such as deuterium or helium nuclei.

Equation Meaning

This summarizes primordial nucleosynthesis. Once the universe cooled enough, protons and neutrons could begin forming the first nuclei, laying the foundation for the chemical history of the cosmos.
