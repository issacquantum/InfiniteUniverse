Logistic Growth

Equation

$$\frac{dN}{dt}=rN\left(1-\frac{N}{K}\right)$$

Literal Reading

dN over dt equals rN of 1 minus N over K.

Common Reading

A population grows quickly when small, then slows as it approaches a carrying capacity.

Symbols

  • \(N\): population size.
  • \(r\): growth rate.
  • \(K\): carrying capacity.
  • \(dN/dt\): rate of population change.

Meaning of the Equation

Growth slows as population approaches carrying capacity.
