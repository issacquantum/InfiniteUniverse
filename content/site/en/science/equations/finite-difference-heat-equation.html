Finite Difference Heat Equation

Equation

$$ u_i^{n+1}=u_i^n+\alpha\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x^2}(u_{i+1}^n-2u_i^n+u_{i-1}^n) $$

Literal Reading

u sub i to the power of n plus 1 equals u sub i to the power of n plus alpha Delta t over Delta x to the power of 2 the quantity u sub i plus 1 to the power of n minus 2u sub i to the power of n plus u sub i minus 1 to the power of n.

Common Reading

This is a discrete update for heat diffusion, replacing continuous spatial curvature with neighboring differences.

Symbols

\(u_i^n\): field value at grid point \(i\) and time step \(n\).

\(\alpha\): diffusion coefficient.

\(\Delta t\): time step.

\(\Delta x\): grid spacing.

Meaning of the Equation

The grid update replaces continuous heat diffusion with neighbor-to-neighbor averaging. It is a numerical approximation whose accuracy depends on resolution and step size.
