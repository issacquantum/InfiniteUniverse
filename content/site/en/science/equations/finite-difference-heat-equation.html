Finite Difference Heat Equation

Equation

$$ u_i^{n+1}=u_i^n+\alpha\frac{\Delta t}{\Delta x^2}(u_{i+1}^n-2u_i^n+u_{i-1}^n) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the next value at grid point i equals the current value plus a diffusion term based on neighboring grid points.

Common Reading

This is a discrete update for heat diffusion, replacing continuous spatial curvature with neighboring differences.

Symbols

\(u_i^n\): field value at grid point \(i\) and time step \(n\).

\(\alpha\): diffusion coefficient.

\(\Delta t\): time step.

\(\Delta x\): grid spacing.

Meaning of the Equation

A discrete update for heat diffusion, replacing continuous spatial curvature with neighboring differences.
