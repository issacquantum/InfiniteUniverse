Trapped Surface Condition

Equation

$$\theta_{(\ell)} < 0$$

Literal Reading

theta sub plus < 0 and theta sub minus < 0.

Common Reading

Outgoing light rays are converging rather than spreading outward.

Symbols

\(\theta_{(\ell)}\): expansion of outgoing null geodesics.

\(< 0\): negative expansion, meaning the light front is shrinking.

Meaning of the Equation

When even outward-directed light converges, escape routes are closing. This is why trapped surfaces are tied to black-hole formation theorems.

Scope and Limits

This is a geometric condition on null congruences. It is powerful because it does not depend on a simple escape-speed picture, but applying it in realistic collapse requires the full spacetime geometry.
