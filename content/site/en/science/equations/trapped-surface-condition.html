Trapped Surface Condition

Equation

$$\theta_{(\ell)} < 0$$

Literal Reading

The expansion theta of the outgoing light direction is less than zero.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist reads this as outgoing light rays are converging rather than spreading outward.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\theta_{(\ell)}\): expansion of outgoing null geodesics.

\(< 0\): negative expansion, meaning the light front is shrinking.

Physical Meaning

A trapped surface signals that gravity has become so strong that even outward directed light is pulled inward. It is one of the clean geometric signs of black hole formation.

Scope and Limit

This is a geometric condition on null congruences. It is powerful because it does not depend on a simple escape-speed picture, but applying it in realistic collapse requires the full spacetime geometry.
