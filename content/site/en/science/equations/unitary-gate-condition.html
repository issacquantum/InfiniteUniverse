Unitary Gate Condition

Equation

$$ U^\dagger U = I $$

Literal Reading

U dagger times U equals the identity operator.

Common Reading

Short version: The condition that an ideal quantum gate preserves inner products, amplitudes, and total probability.

Symbols

\(U\): the quantum gate or circuit operation.

\(U^\dagger\): the conjugate transpose of \(U\), which reverses the operation for a unitary gate.

\(I\): the identity operator, meaning no probability is lost or gained.

Meaning of the Equation

The condition that an ideal quantum gate preserves inner products, amplitudes, and total probability.
