Central Dogma Flow

Expression

$$\mathrm{DNA}\rightarrow\mathrm{RNA}\rightarrow\mathrm{protein}$$

Literal Reading

DNA is transcribed into RNA, and RNA is translated into protein.

Biological Reading

This summarizes the usual information flow from genetic sequence to functional molecular machinery.

Symbol Breakdown

DNA: hereditary nucleic-acid sequence.

RNA: transcribed molecular intermediate and regulator.

Protein: folded molecular product built from amino acids.

Meaning

The expression is useful but simplified. Real cells include regulation, RNA functions, editing, feedback, epigenetics, and environmental context.
