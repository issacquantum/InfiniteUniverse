Central Dogma Flow

Equation

$$\mathrm{DNA}\rightarrow\mathrm{RNA}\rightarrow\mathrm{protein}$$

Plain Meaning

Genetic sequence information is commonly copied from DNA to RNA and then used to build proteins.

Symbols

  • ▣DNA: long-term hereditary sequence storage.
  • ▣RNA: working transcript or regulatory molecule.
  • ▣Protein: folded molecule that often performs cellular work.

How to Read It

Read it as a backbone of molecular information flow.

What It Does Not Say

It is not the whole of biology. Regulation, RNA roles, epigenetics, feedback, and environment matter.
