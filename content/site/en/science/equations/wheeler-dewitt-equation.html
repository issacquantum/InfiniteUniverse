Wheeler-DeWitt Equation

Equation

$$\hat{H}\Psi = 0$$

What the Formula Says

H hat acting on Psi equals zero.

What It Means in Practice

This means a schematic Hamiltonian constraint in canonical quantum gravity.

Symbols

\(\hat{H}\): Hamiltonian constraint operator.

\(\Psi\): wave functional of geometry and matter fields.

Equation Meaning

This equation is schematic. It points to the problem of time because the universal state is constrained rather than evolved by an external time parameter.
