Wheeler-DeWitt Equation

Equation

$$\hat{H}\Psi = 0$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: H hat acting on Psi equals zero.

Common Reading

A schematic Hamiltonian constraint in canonical quantum gravity.

Symbols

\(\hat{H}\): Hamiltonian constraint operator.

\(\Psi\): wave functional of geometry and matter fields.

Meaning of the Equation

This is a schematic quantum-gravity constraint, not an ordinary time-evolution equation. It says the total state is constrained rather than evolved by an external clock, which is why recovering ordinary time requires clocks, correlations, or a semiclassical limit.
