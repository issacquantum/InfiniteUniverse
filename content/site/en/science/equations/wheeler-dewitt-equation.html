Wheeler-DeWitt Equation

Equation

$$\hat{H}\Psi = 0$$

Literal Reading

H hat acting on Psi equals zero.

Common Reading

A schematic Hamiltonian constraint in canonical quantum gravity.

Symbols

\(\hat{H}\): Hamiltonian constraint operator.

\(\Psi\): wave functional of geometry and matter fields.

Meaning of the Equation

The equation removes an external time parameter at the total-state level. Ordinary time must then be recovered from internal clocks, correlations, or a semiclassical approximation.

Scope and Limits

This schematic equation suppresses local Hamiltonian constraints, diffeomorphism constraints, factor-ordering choices, regularization, and the physical-inner-product problem.
