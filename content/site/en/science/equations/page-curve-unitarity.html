Page Curve and Unitarity

Equation

$$S_{\rm rad}(t)\uparrow\ \text{until}\ t_{\rm Page},\qquad S_{\rm rad}(t)\downarrow\ \text{after}$$

What the Formula Says

The radiation entropy rises until the Page time, then falls afterward.

What It Means in Practice

This means the entropy pattern expected if black hole evaporation is unitary.

Symbols

\(S_{\rm rad}\): entanglement entropy of Hawking radiation.

\(t_{\rm Page}\): the time when roughly half the black hole entropy has been radiated.

Why It Matters

If information is preserved, late radiation must be correlated with early radiation. The Page curve is a compact way to state that requirement.

Scope and Limits

This is a schematic entropy curve, not a local emission formula. Its force comes from unitarity and entanglement accounting, while the detailed mechanism for information recovery depends on quantum gravity.
