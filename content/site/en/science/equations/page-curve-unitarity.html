Page Curve and Unitarity

Equation

$$S_{\rm rad}(t)\uparrow\ \text{until}\ t_{\rm Page},\qquad S_{\rm rad}(t)\downarrow\ \text{after}$$

Literal Reading

S sub rad of t rises until the Page time and decreases afterward in a unitary evaporation scenario for an initially pure state.

Common Reading

In a unitary evaporation scenario for an initially pure state, radiation entropy rises at first and decreases after the Page time.

Symbols

\(S_{\rm rad}\): entanglement entropy of Hawking radiation.

\(t_{\rm Page}\): the time when roughly half the black hole entropy has been radiated.

Meaning of the Equation

The curve need not be described as simply falling to zero without specifying the unitary, initially pure-state assumption.

Scope and Limits

This is a schematic entropy curve, not a local emission formula. Its force comes from unitarity and entanglement accounting, while the detailed mechanism for information recovery depends on quantum gravity.
