Cross Entropy Loss

Equation

$$ L=-\sum_i y_i\log(\hat{y}_i) $$

Literal Reading

The loss equals minus the sum of each true label component times the logarithm of the predicted probability for that component.

Physicist's Reading

It penalizes confident wrong predictions strongly and rewards probability mass assigned to the correct class.

Symbol Breakdown

\(L\): loss value.

\(y_i\): true label indicator or target probability.

\(\hat{y}_i\): predicted probability for class \(i\).

Physical Meaning

Cross entropy is a training signal. It tells the model how incompatible its predicted distribution is with the target distribution.
