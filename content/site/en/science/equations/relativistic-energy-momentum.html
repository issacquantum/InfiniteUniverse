Relativistic Relation Between Energy and Momentum

Equation

$$E^2 = (pc)^2 + (mc^2)^2$$

Literal Reading

Energy squared equals the square of the quantity p c plus the square of the quantity m c squared.

Common Reading

Short version: The invariant relation connecting total energy, momentum, and rest mass.

Symbols

\(E\): total relativistic energy.

\(p\): relativistic momentum.

\(m\): rest mass.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

The invariant relation connecting total energy, momentum, and rest mass.
