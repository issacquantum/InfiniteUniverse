Relativistic Relation Between Energy and Momentum

Equation

$$E^2 = (pc)^2 + (mc^2)^2$$

Literal Reading

E to the power of 2 equals of pc to the power of 2 plus of mc to the power of 2 to the power of 2.

Common Reading

The invariant relation connecting total energy, momentum, and rest mass.

Symbols

\(E\): total relativistic energy.

\(p\): relativistic momentum.

\(m\): rest mass.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

Rest mass is the invariant length of the energy-momentum relation. Massless particles satisfy the same relation with zero rest mass, giving E equals pc.
