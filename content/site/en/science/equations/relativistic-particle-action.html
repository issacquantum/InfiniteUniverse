Relativistic Particle Action

Equation

$$S = -mc\int ds = -mc^2\int d\tau$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: s equals negative m c times the integral of d s, which also equals negative m c squared times the integral of d tau.

Common Reading

The action for a free massive relativistic particle.

Symbols

\(S\): action.

\(m\): particle mass.

\(ds\): spacetime line element along the worldline.

\(d\tau\): proper time, with \(ds=c\,d\tau\) for a timelike path.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects geometry, motion, horizons, or gravitational sources. It tells what clocks, distances, curvature, or observers would measure, so the physical claim is tied to spacetime structure rather than to a picture or metaphor.
