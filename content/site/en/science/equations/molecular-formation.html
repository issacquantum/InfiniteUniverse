Molecular Formation

Equation

$$\text{atoms} \rightarrow \text{molecules}$$

Literal Reading

H plus H goes to H2.

Common Reading

Atoms later combine into molecules.

Symbols

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

molecules: bound combinations of atoms.

Meaning of the Equation

Molecules form when atoms bind into stable structures through electromagnetic interactions. This step is later than atom formation and opens the door to chemistry.
