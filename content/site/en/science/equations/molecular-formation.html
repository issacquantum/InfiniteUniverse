Molecular Formation

Equation

$$\text{atoms} \rightarrow \text{molecules}$$

Literal Reading

H plus H goes to H2.

Common Reading

Atoms later combine into molecules.

Symbols

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

molecules: bound combinations of atoms.

Meaning of the Equation

This is schematic; actual primordial \(H_2\) formation proceeds mainly through intermediate ions such as \(H^-\) or \(H_2^+\).
