Molecular Formation

Equation

$$\text{atoms} \rightarrow \text{molecules}$$

Literal Reading

Atoms go to molecules.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist would read it as atoms later combine into molecules.

Symbol Breakdown

atoms: neutral atomic systems.

molecules: bound combinations of atoms.

Physical Meaning

This is a schematic summary rather than a single fundamental law. It marks the transition from atomic matter to chemical complexity, which later enables cooling, clouds, chemistry, and ultimately the structures needed for stars and planets.
