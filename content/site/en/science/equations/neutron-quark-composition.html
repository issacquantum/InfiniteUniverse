Neutron Quark Composition

Equation

$$n = udd$$

Literal Reading

n equals udd.

Common Reading

A neutron is composed of one up quark and two down quarks.

Symbols

\(n\): neutron.

\(u\): up quark.

\(d\): down quark.

Meaning of the Equation

Protons and neutrons are built from specific combinations of up and down quarks.
