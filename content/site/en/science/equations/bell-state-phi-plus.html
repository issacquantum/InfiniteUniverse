Bell State Phi Plus

Equation

$$ |\Phi^{+}\rangle = \frac{|00\rangle + |11\rangle}{\sqrt{2}} $$

Literal Reading

vertical bar Φ plus > equals of vertical bar 00 > plus vertical bar 11 > over square root of 2.

Common Reading

One of the maximally entangled Bell states, where two qubits are equally shared between the outcomes zero zero and one one.

Symbols

\(|\Phi^{+}\rangle\): the specific Bell state being named.

\(|00\rangle\): both qubits in state zero.

\(|11\rangle\): both qubits in state one.

\(\sqrt{2}\): the normalization factor that keeps the total probability equal to one.

\(+\): the two basis states add coherently with the same relative phase.

Meaning of the Equation

Bell states are maximally entangled two-qubit states.
