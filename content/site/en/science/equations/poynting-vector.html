Poynting Vector

Equation

$$\mathbf{S}=\frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathbf{E}\times\mathbf{B}$$

Plain Meaning

Electromagnetic energy flows in the direction given by the cross product of the electric and magnetic fields.

Symbols

  • ▣\(\mathbf{S}\): energy-flow vector.
  • ▣\(\mathbf{E}\): electric field.
  • ▣\(\mathbf{B}\): magnetic field.
  • ▣\(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.

How to Read It

Read the arrow of \(\mathbf{S}\) as the direction energy moves; its size gives energy flow per area per time.

What It Does Not Say

It does not describe matter transport. It describes field energy transport.
