Poynting Vector

Equation

$$\mathbf{S}=\frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathbf{E}\times\mathbf{B}$$

Literal Reading

S equals open parenthesis 1 over mu 0 close parenthesis open parenthesis E cross B close parenthesis.

Common Reading

The electromagnetic energy-flow vector equals one over mu naught times E cross B.

Symbols

  • \(\mathbf{S}\): energy-flow vector.
  • \(\mathbf{E}\): electric field.
  • \(\mathbf{B}\): magnetic field.
  • \(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.

Meaning of the Equation

It tells the direction and rate at which electromagnetic energy flows through space.
