Poynting Vector

Equation

$$\mathbf{S}=\frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathbf{E}\times\mathbf{B}$$

Literal Reading

S equals of 1 over mu 0 the quantity E cross B.

Common Reading

The electromagnetic energy-flow vector equals one over mu naught times E cross B.

Symbols

  • \(\mathbf{S}\): energy-flow vector.
  • \(\mathbf{E}\): electric field.
  • \(\mathbf{B}\): magnetic field.
  • \(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.

Meaning of the Equation

The vector points where electromagnetic energy travels and measures how much power crosses each square meter. It turns electric and magnetic field geometry into an energy-flow picture.
