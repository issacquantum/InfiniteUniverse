Poynting Vector

Equation

$$\mathbf{S}=\frac{1}{\mu_0}\mathbf{E}\times\mathbf{B}$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: S equals one over mu naught times E cross B.

Common Reading

A short way to say it: Electromagnetic energy flows in the direction given by the cross product of the electric and magnetic fields.

Symbols

  • \(\mathbf{S}\): energy-flow vector.
  • \(\mathbf{E}\): electric field.
  • \(\mathbf{B}\): magnetic field.
  • \(\mu_0\): vacuum permeability.

Meaning of the Equation

Electromagnetic energy flows in the direction given by the cross product of the electric and magnetic fields.
