Hubble's Law

Equation

$$v = H_0 d$$

Literal Reading

v equals H naught times d.

Common Reading

Short version: Recessional velocity is proportional to distance, with proportionality constant H naught.

Symbols

\(v\): recessional velocity.

\(H_0\): the present-day Hubble constant.

\(d\): distance to the galaxy or source.

Meaning of the Equation

Recessional velocity is proportional to distance, with proportionality constant H naught.
