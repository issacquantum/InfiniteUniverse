No Cloning and Linearity

Equation

$$ U\left(|\psi\rangle|0\rangle\right) = |\psi\rangle|\psi\rangle $$

Literal Reading

U vertical bar psi > vertical bar 0 > equals vertical bar psi > vertical bar psi >.

Common Reading

The kind of operation a perfect universal quantum copier would need to perform.

Symbols

\(U\): a hypothetical unitary operation that would perform the copying.

\(|\psi\rangle\): an arbitrary unknown quantum state.

\(|0\rangle\): a blank target state prepared to receive the copy.

\(|\psi\rangle|\psi\rangle\): two identical copies of the original state.

Meaning of the Equation

If a universal copier worked on arbitrary superpositions, linearity would produce contradictions. Unknown quantum states cannot be copied like classical bit strings.
