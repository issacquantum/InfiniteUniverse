Euler-Lagrange Equation

Equation

$$\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot q}-\frac{\partial L}{\partial q}=0$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the time derivative of partial L over partial q dot minus partial L over partial q equals zero.

Common Reading

This is the rule that turns the action principle into an equation of motion.

Symbols

  • \(L\): Lagrangian.
  • \(q\): coordinate.
  • \(\dot q\): velocity-like rate of change.
  • \(t\): time.

Meaning of the Equation

The rule that turns the action principle into an equation of motion.
