Euler-Lagrange Equation

Equation

$$\frac{d}{dt}\frac{\partial L}{\partial \dot q}-\frac{\partial L}{\partial q}=0$$

Plain Meaning

This is the rule that turns the action principle into an equation of motion.

Symbols

  • ▣\(L\): Lagrangian.
  • ▣\(q\): coordinate.
  • ▣\(\dot q\): velocity-like rate of change.
  • ▣\(t\): time.

How to Read It

Write the Lagrangian, apply this equation, and the path of the system follows from the energy structure.

What It Does Not Say

It is not a new force. It is another way to derive the same classical motion when the assumptions fit.
