Scalar Field Lagrangian

Equation

$$\mathcal{L}=\frac{1}{2}\partial_\mu\phi\,\partial^\mu\phi-\frac{1}{2}m^2\phi^2$$

Literal Reading

The Lagrangian density equals a kinetic field term minus a mass term.

Physicist's Reading

It is the compact field-theory rule for a simple free scalar field.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\mathcal{L}\): Lagrangian density.

\(\phi\): scalar field.

\(\partial_\mu\): spacetime derivative.

\(m\): field mass parameter.

Physical Meaning

The first term describes field variation through spacetime. The second term gives the field a mass scale. Interactions would require additional terms.
