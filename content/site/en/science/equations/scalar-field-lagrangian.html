Scalar Field Lagrangian

Equation

$$\mathcal{L}=\frac{1}{2}\partial_\mu\phi\,\partial^\mu\phi-\frac{1}{2}m^2\phi^2$$

Literal Reading

ℒ equals 1 over 2 partial sub mu phi partial to the power of mu phi minus 1 over 2 m to the power of 2 phi to the power of 2 minus V open parenthesis phi close parenthesis.

Common Reading

This is the basic rule for a free scalar field: one term tracks field variation, the other gives a mass scale.

Symbols

  • \(\mathcal{L}\): Lagrangian density.
  • \(\phi\): scalar field.
  • \(\partial_\mu\): spacetime derivative.
  • \(m\): mass parameter.

Meaning of the Equation

The Lagrangian density defines the field dynamics and interactions.
