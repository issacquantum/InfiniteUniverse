Closed Timelike Curve Condition

Equation

$$ \Delta t_{\rm mouths} > \frac{L}{c} $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: if the time shift between mouths exceeds the light-travel time through ordinary space, a closed timelike route can appear.

Common Reading

A traversable wormhole with differently aged mouths can become a time-machine spacetime unless chronology protection prevents it.

Symbols

\(\Delta t_{\rm mouths}\): time offset between the two wormhole mouths.

\(L\): ordinary spatial separation between mouths.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects geometry, motion, horizons, or gravitational sources. It tells what clocks, distances, curvature, or observers would measure, so the physical claim is tied to spacetime structure rather than to a picture or metaphor.
