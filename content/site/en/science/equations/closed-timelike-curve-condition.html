Closed Timelike Curve Condition

Equation

$$ \Delta t_{\rm mouths} > \frac{L}{c} $$

Literal Reading

ds to the power of 2 < 0 along a closed curve.

Common Reading

A traversable wormhole with differently aged mouths can become a time-machine spacetime unless chronology protection prevents it.

Symbols

\(\Delta t_{\rm mouths}\): time offset between the two wormhole mouths.

\(L\): ordinary spatial separation between mouths.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

The condition marks a possible causal loop, where an observer's path can return to its own earlier time. That is why stability and chronology protection matter before treating such geometry as physical.
