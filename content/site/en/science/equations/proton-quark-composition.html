Proton Quark Composition

Equation

$$p = uud$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: p equals uud.

Common Reading

A proton is composed of two up quarks and one down quark.

Symbols

\(p\): proton.

\(u\): up quark.

\(d\): down quark.

Meaning of the Equation

Protons and neutrons are built from specific combinations of up and down quarks.
