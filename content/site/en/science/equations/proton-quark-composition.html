Proton Quark Composition

Equation

$$p = uud$$

What the Formula Says

p equals u u d.

What It Means in Practice

This means a proton is composed of two up quarks and one down quark.

Symbols

\(p\): proton.

\(u\): up quark.

\(d\): down quark.

Why It Matters

This is the quark-content shorthand for the proton in the quark model. The proton is a baryon made of three valence quarks bound together by the strong interaction.
