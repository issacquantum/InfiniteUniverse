Proton Quark Composition

Equation

$$p = uud$$

Literal Reading

p equals u u d.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist would read it as a proton is composed of two up quarks and one down quark.

Symbol Breakdown

\(p\): proton.

\(u\): up quark.

\(d\): down quark.

Physical Meaning

This is the quark-content shorthand for the proton in the quark model. The proton is a baryon made of three valence quarks bound together by the strong interaction.
