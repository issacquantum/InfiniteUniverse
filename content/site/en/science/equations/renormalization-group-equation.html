Renormalization Group Equation

Equation

$$\mu\frac{dg}{d\mu}=\beta(g)$$

Literal Reading

Mu times d g over d mu equals beta of g.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist reads this as the scale dependence of a coupling constant.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\mu\): energy or renormalization scale.

\(g\): coupling parameter.

\(\beta(g)\): beta function controlling how \(g\) runs with scale.

Physical Meaning

Renormalization shows that physical descriptions depend on scale. It is not just a trick to hide infinities; it is part of how modern field theory understands effective physics.
