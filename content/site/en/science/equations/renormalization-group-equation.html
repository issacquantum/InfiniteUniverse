Renormalization Group Equation

Equation

$$\mu\frac{dg}{d\mu}=\beta(g)$$

Literal Reading

mu d g over d mu equals beta of g.

Common Reading

A coupling can change when the scale of the measurement changes.

Symbols

  • \(\mu\): energy or length scale used to define the theory.
  • \(g\): coupling parameter.
  • \(\beta(g)\): function telling how the coupling runs with scale.

Meaning of the Equation

The beta function tells how a coupling changes with energy scale. It is the mathematical language for scale dependence in quantum field theory.
