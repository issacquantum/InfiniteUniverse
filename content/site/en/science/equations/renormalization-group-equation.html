Renormalization Group Equation

Equation

$$\mu\frac{dg}{d\mu}=\beta(g)$$

Plain Meaning

A coupling can change when the scale of the measurement changes.

Symbols

  • \(\mu\): energy or length scale used to define the theory.
  • \(g\): coupling parameter.
  • \(\beta(g)\): function telling how the coupling runs with scale.

How to Read It

Read it as scale dependence: the same theory can look different when probed at different resolutions.

What It Does Not Say

It does not mean the theory is arbitrary. It tells how predictions remain controlled across scales.
