Lorentz Force Law

Equation

$$\mathbf{F}=q(\mathbf{E}+\mathbf{v}\times\mathbf{B})$$

Literal Reading

F equals q open parenthesis E plus v cross B close parenthesis.

Common Reading

A charge experiences force from the electric field and from motion through the magnetic field.

Symbols

  • \(q\): electric charge.
  • \(\mathbf{E}\): electric field.
  • \(\mathbf{v}\): particle velocity.
  • \(\mathbf{B}\): magnetic field.

Meaning of the Equation

Electric fields push charges directly; magnetic fields push moving charges sideways.
