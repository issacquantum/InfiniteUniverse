Gravitational Redshift

Equation

$$\frac{\Delta f}{f} = \frac{\Delta\Phi}{c^2}$$

Common Reading

The fractional frequency shift caused by a difference in gravitational potential in the weak field limit.

Symbols

\(\Delta f\): change in measured frequency.

\(f\): reference frequency.

\(\Delta\Phi\): difference in gravitational potential.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

Light climbing out of gravity loses frequency and is redshifted.
