Gravitational Redshift

Equation

$$\frac{\Delta f}{f} = \frac{\Delta\Phi}{c^2}$$

Literal Reading

Delta f over f equals delta gravitational potential over c squared.

Common Reading

In the weak-field approximation, a frequency shift is proportional to the change in gravitational potential.

Symbols

\(\Delta f\): change in measured frequency.

\(f\): reference frequency.

\(\Delta\Phi\): difference in gravitational potential.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

This is not the full Schwarzschild redshift formula; it is the weak-field limit.
