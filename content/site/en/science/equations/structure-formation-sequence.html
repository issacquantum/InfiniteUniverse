Structure Formation

Equation

$$\text{molecules} \rightarrow \text{stars, planets, and matter structures}$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: molecules go to stars, planets, and matter structures.

Common Reading

Molecular matter eventually participates in the formation of stars, planets, and larger material structures.

Symbols

molecules: chemically bound atomic systems.

stars, planets, and matter structures: larger cosmic structures assembled over time.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation states how the named quantities depend on each other. Its purpose is to turn a conceptual statement into a rule that can be calculated, compared, or tested inside the model where it is used.
