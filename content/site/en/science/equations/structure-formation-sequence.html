Structure Formation

Equation

$$\text{molecules} \rightarrow \text{stars, planets, and matter structures}$$

Literal Reading

delta rho over rho grows under gravity.

Common Reading

Molecular matter eventually participates in the formation of stars, planets, and larger material structures.

Symbols

molecules: chemically bound atomic systems.

stars, planets, and matter structures: larger cosmic structures assembled over time.

Meaning of the Equation

Small density variations grow gravitationally into large cosmic structures.
