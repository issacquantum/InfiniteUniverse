First Law of Thermodynamics

Equation

$$dU=\delta Q-\delta W$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the change in internal energy equals heat added minus work done by the system.

Common Reading

A short way to say it: Energy inside a system changes when heat enters or when the system does work.

Symbols

  • \(dU\): change in internal energy.
  • \(\delta Q\): heat added to the system.
  • \(\delta W\): work done by the system.

Meaning of the Equation

Energy inside a system changes when heat enters or when the system does work.
