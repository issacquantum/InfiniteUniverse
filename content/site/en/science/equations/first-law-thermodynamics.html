First Law of Thermodynamics

Equation

$$dU=\delta Q-\delta W$$

Plain Meaning

Energy inside a system changes when heat enters or when the system does work.

Symbols

  • \(dU\): change in internal energy.
  • \(\delta Q\): heat added to the system.
  • \(\delta W\): work done by the system.

How to Read It

It is energy accounting. Heat and work are transfer processes; internal energy is the state quantity.

What It Does Not Say

It does not explain by itself why processes have a direction. That needs entropy and the second law.
