First Law of Thermodynamics

Equation

$$dU=\delta Q-\delta W$$

Literal Reading

The change in internal energy equals heat added minus work done by the system.

Physicist's Reading

This is energy conservation written for thermodynamic systems, separating heat transfer from mechanical work.

Symbol Breakdown

\(dU\): change in internal energy.

\(\delta Q\): heat added to the system.

\(\delta W\): work done by the system.

Physical Meaning

It tracks energy accounting but does not by itself explain why entropy increases.
