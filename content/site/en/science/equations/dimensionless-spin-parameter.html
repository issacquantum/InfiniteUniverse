Dimensionless Spin Parameter

Equation

$$ a_* = \frac{cJ}{GM^2} $$

Literal Reading

a star equals c J divided by G M squared.

Common Reading

The normalized spin of a Kerr black hole.

Symbols

\(a_*\): dimensionless spin parameter.

\(J\): angular momentum.

\(M\): black hole mass.

\(G\): gravitational constant.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

For Kerr black holes, \(0\le |a_*|\le1\) in classical general relativity. Values outside that range would not describe an ordinary Kerr black-hole horizon.

Scope and Limits

The parameter is defined for Kerr-like compact objects. Measurements of \(a_*\) depend on model assumptions about disks, spectra, or gravitational waves, so the number is physical but not directly visible by itself.
