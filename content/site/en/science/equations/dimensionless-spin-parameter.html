Dimensionless Spin Parameter

Equation

$$\chi = \frac{cJ}{GM^2}$$

Literal Reading

a sub times equals cJ over open parenthesis GM to the power of 2 close parenthesis.

Common Reading

The normalized spin of a Kerr black hole.

Symbols

\(\chi\): dimensionless spin parameter.

\(J\): angular momentum.

\(M\): black hole mass.

\(G\): gravitational constant.

\(c\): speed of light.

Meaning of the Equation

Rotation changes the horizon structure and is measured by angular momentum.

Scope and Limits

The parameter is defined for Kerr-like compact objects. Measurements of \(\chi\) depend on model assumptions about disks, spectra, or gravitational waves, so the number is physical but not directly visible by itself.
