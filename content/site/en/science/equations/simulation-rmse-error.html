Simulation RMSE (root mean square error)

Equation

$$ RMSE=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_{i=1}^{N}(y_i-\hat{y}_i)^2} $$

What the Formula Says

Root mean square error is the square root of the average squared difference between reference values and simulated values.

What It Means in Practice

This gives one compact measure of how far a simulation is from the data or reference solution it claims to reproduce.

Symbols

\(y_i\): reference or measured value.

\(\hat{y}_i\): simulated value.

\(N\): number of compared points.

Equation Meaning

RMSE is not the whole validation story, but it is a useful numerical check against pretending that a visual match is enough.
