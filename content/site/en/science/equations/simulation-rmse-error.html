Simulation RMSE (root mean square error)

Equation

$$ \mathrm{RMSE}=\sqrt{\frac{1}{N}\sum_i(\hat y_i-y_i)^2} $$

Literal Reading

RMSE equals the square root of one over N times the sum over i of y hat sub i minus y sub i squared.

Common Reading

RMSE is the square root of the mean squared prediction error.

Symbols

\(y_i\): reference or measured value.

\(\hat{y}_i\): simulated value.

\(N\): number of compared points.

Meaning of the Equation

Squaring makes large misses count more strongly. Taking the square root returns the error to the same units as the simulated quantity.
