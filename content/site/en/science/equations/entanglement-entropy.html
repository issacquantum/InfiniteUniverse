Entanglement Entropy

Equation

$$ S(\rho_A) = S(\rho_B) $$

Literal Reading

S sub A equals minus trace open parenthesis rho sub A log rho sub A close parenthesis.

Common Reading

Entropy measures uncertainty, disorder, or missing information in the relevant physical or informational state.

Symbols

\(\rho_A\): the reduced density matrix for subsystem \(A\).

\(\rho_B\): the reduced density matrix for subsystem \(B\).

\(S\): von Neumann entropy.

Meaning of the Equation

Classical randomized linear-algebra methods can match some claimed quantum speedups under similar data access assumptions.
