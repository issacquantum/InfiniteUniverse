Entanglement Entropy

Equation

$$ S(\rho_A) = S(\rho_B) $$

Literal Reading

The entropy of subsystem A equals the entropy of subsystem B.

Common Reading

Short version: A pure bipartite entangled state gives both subsystems the same amount of reduced entropy.

Symbols

\(\rho_A\): the reduced density matrix for subsystem \(A\).

\(\rho_B\): the reduced density matrix for subsystem \(B\).

\(S\): von Neumann entropy.

Meaning of the Equation

A pure bipartite entangled state gives both subsystems the same amount of reduced entropy.
