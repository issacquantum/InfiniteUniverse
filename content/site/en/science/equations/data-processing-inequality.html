Data Processing Inequality

Equation

$$ D(\mathcal{E}(\rho)\Vert\mathcal{E}(\sigma)) \le D(\rho\Vert\sigma) $$

Literal Reading

D of E of rho relative to E of sigma is less than or equal to D of rho relative to sigma.

Common Reading

Applying the same quantum channel cannot increase quantum relative entropy between two states.

Symbols

\(D\): quantum relative entropy.

\(\mathcal{E}\): quantum channel.

\(\rho,\sigma\): density matrices being compared.

Meaning of the Equation

The channel can discard or blur distinguishability, but it cannot create extra distinguishability between \(\rho\) and \(\sigma\).
