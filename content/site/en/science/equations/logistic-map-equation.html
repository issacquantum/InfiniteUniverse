Equation

$$x_{n+1}=r x_n(1-x_n)$$

Plain Meaning

The next value is produced from the current value by a simple nonlinear rule.

Symbols

  • \(x_n\): current value.
  • \(x_{n+1}\): next value.
  • \(r\): control parameter.

How to Read It

Changing \(r\) can move the system from stability to cycles to chaos.

Meaning in Context

The equation is a compact nonlinear update rule that can show fixed points, cycles, and chaos, useful as a teaching model of nonlinear dynamics.
