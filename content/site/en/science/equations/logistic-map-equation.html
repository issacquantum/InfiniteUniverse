Logistic Map

Equation

$$x_{n+1}=r x_n(1-x_n)$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the next x equals r times the current x times one minus the current x.

Common Reading

A short way to say it: The next value is produced from the current value by a simple nonlinear rule.

Symbols

  • \(x_n\): current value.
  • \(x_{n+1}\): next value.
  • \(r\): control parameter.

Meaning of the Equation
