Logistic Map

Equation

$$x_{n+1}=r x_n(1-x_n)$$

Plain Meaning

The next value is produced from the current value by a simple nonlinear rule.

Symbols

  • ▣\(x_n\): current value.
  • ▣\(x_{n+1}\): next value.
  • ▣\(r\): control parameter.

How to Read It

Changing \(r\) can move the system from stability to cycles to chaos.

What It Does Not Say

It is not a full population model by itself. It is a teaching model for nonlinear dynamics.
