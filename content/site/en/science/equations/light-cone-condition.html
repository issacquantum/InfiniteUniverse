Light Cone Condition

Equation

$$ds^2 = 0$$

Literal Reading

ds to the power of 2 equals 0.

Common Reading

The condition for lightlike separation between nearby events.

Symbols

\(ds^2\): spacetime interval.

\(0\): the boundary between timelike and spacelike separation.

Meaning of the Equation

The zero interval is the boundary of causal contact for light. Events inside, on, or outside the cone have different causal relationships, so the equation organizes what can influence what.
