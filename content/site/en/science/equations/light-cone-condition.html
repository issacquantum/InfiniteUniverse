Light Cone Condition

Equation

$$ds^2 = 0$$

Literal Reading

d s squared equals zero.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist reads this as the condition for lightlike separation between nearby events.

Symbol Breakdown

\(ds^2\): spacetime interval.

\(0\): the boundary between timelike and spacelike separation.

Physical Meaning

Events with \(ds^2=0\) lie on a light cone. Events inside the cone can be causally connected by slower-than-light motion, while events outside it cannot influence each other without faster-than-light signaling.
