Einstein Field Equations with Lambda

Equation

$$G_{\mu\nu}+\Lambda g_{\mu\nu}=\frac{8\pi G}{c^4}T_{\mu\nu}$$

Literal Reading

G mu nu plus Lambda times g mu nu equals eight pi G over c to the fourth times T mu nu.

Common Reading

Short version: Spacetime curvature, including the cosmological constant term, being related to matter and energy.

Symbols

\(G_{\mu\nu}\): Einstein tensor, encoding curvature.

\(\Lambda\): cosmological constant.

\(g_{\mu\nu}\): metric tensor.

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

Meaning of the Equation

Spacetime curvature, including the cosmological constant term, being related to matter and energy.
