Einstein Field Equations with Lambda

Equation

$$G_{\mu\nu}+\Lambda g_{\mu\nu}=\frac{8\pi G}{c^4}T_{\mu\nu}$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: G sub open brace mu nu close brace plus Lambda g sub open brace mu nu close brace equals open parenthesis 8 pi G over c to the power of 4 close parenthesis T sub open brace mu nu close brace.

Common Reading

Spacetime curvature is related to the stress-energy content of matter and radiation.

Symbols

\(G_{\mu\nu}\): Einstein tensor, encoding curvature.

\(\Lambda\): cosmological constant.

\(g_{\mu\nu}\): metric tensor.

\(T_{\mu\nu}\): stress energy tensor.

Meaning of the Equation

It connects the quantities in the formula so the relation can be used to compute or interpret a situation in Relativity & Spacetime.
