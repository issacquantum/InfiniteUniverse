Gradient Descent Update

Equation

$$ \theta_{t+1}=\theta_t-\eta\nabla_\theta L(\theta_t) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: theta sub open brace t plus 1 close brace equals theta sub t minus eta nabla L open parenthesis theta sub t close parenthesis.

Common Reading

Parameters are updated by moving opposite the gradient of the loss.

Symbols

\(\theta_t\): parameters at training step \(t\).

\(\eta\): learning rate.

\(L\): loss function being minimized.

\(\nabla_\theta L\): gradient of the loss with respect to the parameters.

Meaning of the Equation

It connects the quantities in the formula so the relation can be used to compute or interpret a situation in Artificial Intelligence.
