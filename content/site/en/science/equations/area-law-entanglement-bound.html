Area-Law Entanglement Bound

Equation

$$ S(A)=O(|\partial A|) $$

Literal Reading

S of A proportional to vertical bar partial A vertical bar.

Common Reading

Entanglement scales with the boundary, not the full volume.

Symbols

\(S(A)\): entanglement entropy of region \(A\).

\(\partial A\): the boundary of region \(A\).

\(|\partial A|\): size or area of that boundary.

\(O(\cdot)\): scaling up to constant factors.

Meaning of the Equation

Classical randomized linear-algebra methods can match some claimed quantum speedups under similar data access assumptions.
