Higgs Vacuum Expectation Value

Equation

$$\langle H \rangle \neq 0$$

Literal Reading

The expectation value of H is not equal to zero.

Common Reading

Short version: The Higgs field acquires a nonzero vacuum expectation value.

Symbols

\(\langle H \rangle\): vacuum expectation value of the Higgs field.

\(\neq 0\): not zero.

Meaning of the Equation

The Higgs field acquires a nonzero vacuum expectation value.
