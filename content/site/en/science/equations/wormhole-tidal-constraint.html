Traversability Tidal Constraint

Equation

$$ |\Delta a| \lesssim g_\oplus $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: the tidal acceleration across the traveler should be no larger than roughly Earth gravity.

Common Reading

Passing through is not enough.

Symbols

\(\Delta a\): differential acceleration between nearby parts of the traveler.

\(g_\oplus\): gravitational acceleration at Earth's surface, used as a human-scale reference.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects geometry, motion, horizons, or gravitational sources. It tells what clocks, distances, curvature, or observers would measure, so the physical claim is tied to spacetime structure rather than to a picture or metaphor.
