Traversability Tidal Constraint

Equation

$$ |\Delta a| \lesssim g_\oplus $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: vertical bar R sub open brace \hat0\hat i\hat0\hat j close brace vertical bar ℓ < g sub earth.

Common Reading

Passing through is not enough.

Symbols

\(\Delta a\): differential acceleration between nearby parts of the traveler.

\(g_\oplus\): gravitational acceleration at Earth's surface, used as a human-scale reference.

Meaning of the Equation

Gravity pulls more strongly on the near side than the far side, stretching objects.
