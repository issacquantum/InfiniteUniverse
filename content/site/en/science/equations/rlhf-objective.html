RLHF (reinforcement learning from human feedback) Objective

Equation

$$ \max_\theta\;\mathbb{E}[r_\phi(x,y)]-\beta D_{\rm KL}(\pi_\theta(y|x)\|\pi_{\rm ref}(y|x)) $$

Literal Reading

J of theta equals E open bracket R of x comma y close bracket minus beta KL of pi sub theta norm pi sub ref.

Common Reading

Maximize reward-model score while penalizing deviation from a reference policy.

Symbols

\(r_\phi(x,y)\): learned reward model score for prompt \(x\) and output \(y\).

\(\pi_\theta\): tuned policy.

\(\pi_{\rm ref}\): reference model.

\(\beta\): strength of the KL penalty.

Meaning of the Equation

The reward term pushes behavior toward preferred outputs, while the KL penalty limits drift from the reference model. This reduces but does not eliminate reward hacking.
