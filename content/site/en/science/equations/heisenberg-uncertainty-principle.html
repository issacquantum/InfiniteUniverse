Heisenberg Uncertainty Principle

Equation

$$ \Delta x \, \Delta p \geq \frac{\hbar}{2} $$

Literal Reading

Delta x Delta p greater than or equal to h minus bar over 2.

Common Reading

Position uncertainty times momentum uncertainty cannot be smaller than h-bar over two.

Symbols

\(\Delta x\): the uncertainty or spread in position.

\(\Delta p\): the uncertainty or spread in momentum.

\(\hbar/2\): the minimum quantum bound for the product of those spreads.

Meaning of the Equation

Some pairs of quantities cannot both be known with unlimited precision.
