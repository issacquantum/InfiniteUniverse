Hadamard Gate Matrix

Equation

$$ H = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{pmatrix} $$

Literal Reading

Common Reading

The Hadamard gate turns basis states into balanced superpositions.

Symbols

\(H\): the Hadamard gate.

\(\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\): the normalization factor that keeps the operation unitary.

\(\begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{pmatrix}\): the matrix form of the transformation in the \(|0\rangle, |1\rangle\) basis.

Meaning of the Equation

Hadamard is a basic way to create interference-ready amplitudes. Later gates can make those amplitudes add or cancel.
