Holevo Information

Equation

$$ \chi = S\!\left(\sum_x p_x\rho_x\right) - \sum_x p_x S(\rho_x) $$

Literal Reading

Read it as: χ equals S open parenthesis sum sub x p sub x rho sub x close parenthesis minus sum sub x p sub x S open parenthesis rho sub x close parenthesis.

Common Reading

Chi equals the entropy of the average state minus the average entropy of the states.

Symbols

\(\chi\): the Holevo information.

\(p_x\): the probability of preparing state \(\rho_x\).

\(\rho_x\): one possible quantum state in the ensemble.

\(\sum_x p_x\rho_x\): the average quantum state.

\(S\): von Neumann entropy.

Meaning of the Equation

It bounds how much classical information can be extracted from quantum states.
