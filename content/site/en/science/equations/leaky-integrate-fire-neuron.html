Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Neuron

Equation

$$\tau_m\frac{dV}{dt}=-(V-E_L)+RI(t)$$

Literal Reading

τ sub m dV over dt equals minus open parenthesis V minus V sub rest close parenthesis plus R I open parenthesis t close parenthesis.

Common Reading

A model neuron accumulates input voltage but leaks back toward a resting level over time.

Symbols

  • \(V\): membrane voltage.
  • \(\tau_m\): membrane time constant.
  • \(E_L\): leak or resting voltage.
  • \(R\): resistance.
  • \(I(t)\): input current.

Meaning of the Equation

Membrane voltage integrates input current while leaking back toward resting voltage.
