Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Neuron

Equation

$$\tau_m\frac{dV}{dt}=-(V-E_L)+RI(t)$$

Plain Meaning

A model neuron accumulates input voltage but leaks back toward a resting level over time.

Symbols

  • ▣\(V\): membrane voltage.
  • ▣\(\tau_m\): membrane time constant.
  • ▣\(E_L\): leak or resting voltage.
  • ▣\(R\): resistance.
  • ▣\(I(t)\): input current.

How to Read It

If voltage reaches a threshold, the model emits a spike and resets. This captures excitability in a simplified way.

What It Does Not Say

It leaves out real ion-channel dynamics, dendrites, neurotransmitters, plasticity, and network context.
