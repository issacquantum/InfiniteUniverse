Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Neuron

Equation

$$\tau_m\frac{dV}{dt}=-(V-E_L)+RI(t)$$

Literal Reading

τ sub m dV over dt equals minus of V minus V sub rest plus R I of t.

Common Reading

A model neuron accumulates input voltage but leaks back toward a resting level over time.

Symbols

  • \(V\): membrane voltage.
  • \(\tau_m\): membrane time constant.
  • \(E_L\): leak or resting voltage.
  • \(R\): resistance.
  • \(I(t)\): input current.

Meaning of the Equation

The leak makes old input fade instead of accumulating forever. The threshold turns enough recent input into a spike, giving a simple model of neural firing.
