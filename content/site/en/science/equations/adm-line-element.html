ADM (Arnowitt-Deser-Misner) Line Element

Equation

$$ds^2 = -N^2c^2dt^2 + h_{ij}(dx^i + N^i dt)(dx^j + N^j dt)$$

Literal Reading

d s squared is written using the lapse N, the shift N i, and the spatial metric h i j.

Common Reading

Spacetime split into spatial slices evolving from one label time to another.

Symbols

\(N\): lapse, controlling separation between slices.

\(N^i\): shift, controlling how spatial coordinates slide between slices.

\(h_{ij}\): metric on each spatial slice.

Meaning of the Equation

ADM rewrites spacetime as a stack of spatial slices. The lapse says how much proper time separates slices, the shift says how coordinates slide between slices, and the spatial metric gives the geometry within each slice. This is a decomposition choice, not a preferred universal time.
