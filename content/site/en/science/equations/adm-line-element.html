ADM (Arnowitt-Deser-Misner) Line Element

Equation

$$ds^2 = -N^2c^2dt^2 + h_{ij}(dx^i + N^i dt)(dx^j + N^j dt)$$

What the Formula Says

d s squared is written using lapse N, shift N i, and the spatial metric h i j.

What It Means in Practice

This means spacetime split into spatial slices evolving from one label time to another.

Symbols

\(N\): lapse, controlling separation between slices.

\(N^i\): shift, controlling how spatial coordinates slide between slices.

\(h_{ij}\): metric on each spatial slice.

Equation Meaning

The ADM split is the doorway to Hamiltonian general relativity and canonical quantum gravity. It also shows why time in gravity is partly a choice of slicing.
