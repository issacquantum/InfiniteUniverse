ADM (Arnowitt-Deser-Misner) Line Element

Equation

$$ds^2 = -N^2c^2dt^2 + h_{ij}(dx^i + N^i dt)(dx^j + N^j dt)$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: d s squared is written using lapse N, shift N i, and the spatial metric h i j.

Common Reading

Spacetime split into spatial slices evolving from one label time to another.

Symbols

\(N\): lapse, controlling separation between slices.

\(N^i\): shift, controlling how spatial coordinates slide between slices.

\(h_{ij}\): metric on each spatial slice.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation connects geometry, motion, horizons, or gravitational sources. It tells what clocks, distances, curvature, or observers would measure, so the physical claim is tied to spacetime structure rather than to a picture or metaphor.
