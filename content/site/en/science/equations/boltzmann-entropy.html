Boltzmann Entropy

Equation

$$S_B=k_B\log W$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: Boltzmann entropy equals Boltzmann's constant times the logarithm of W.

Common Reading

Entropy counts how many microstates match the same macrostate.

Symbols

  • \(S_B\): Boltzmann entropy.
  • \(k_B\): Boltzmann constant.
  • \(W\): number of compatible microstates.

Meaning of the Equation

This relation measures uncertainty, correlation, capacity, or distinguishability. It tells how much information is present, lost, shared, or recoverable, so the concept becomes a quantity that can be compared rather than just described in words.
