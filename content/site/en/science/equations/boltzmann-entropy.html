Boltzmann Entropy

Equation

$$S_B = k_B \log W$$

Literal Reading

Boltzmann entropy equals Boltzmann's constant times the logarithm of W.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist reads this as entropy measuring how many microscopic arrangements correspond to the same macroscopic description.

Symbol Breakdown

\(S_B\): Boltzmann entropy.

\(k_B\): Boltzmann constant.

\(W\): number or phase space volume of compatible microstates.

Physical Meaning

Entropy grows statistically because high entropy macrostates correspond to vastly more microstates than low entropy macrostates. This is central to the arrow of time.
