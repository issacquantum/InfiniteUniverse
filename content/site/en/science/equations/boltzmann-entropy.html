Boltzmann Entropy

Equation

$$S_B=k_B\log W$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: Boltzmann entropy equals Boltzmann's constant times the logarithm of W.

Common Reading

A short way to say it: Entropy grows with the number of microscopic arrangements compatible with the same large-scale description.

Symbols

  • \(S_B\): Boltzmann entropy.
  • \(k_B\): Boltzmann constant.
  • \(W\): number of compatible microstates.

Meaning of the Equation

Entropy grows with the number of microscopic arrangements compatible with the same large-scale description.
