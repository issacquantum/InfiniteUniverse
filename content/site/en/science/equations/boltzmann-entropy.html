Boltzmann Entropy

Equation

$$S_B=k_B\log W$$

Literal Reading

Read it as: S equals k sub B natural log omega.

Common Reading

Entropy measures uncertainty, disorder, or missing information in the relevant physical or informational state.

Symbols

  • \(S_B\): Boltzmann entropy.
  • \(k_B\): Boltzmann constant.
  • \(W\): number of compatible microstates.

Meaning of the Equation

Macroscopic entropy counts how many microscopic arrangements match the same visible state.
