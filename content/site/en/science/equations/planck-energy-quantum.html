Planck Energy Quantum

Equation

$$ E = h\nu $$

Literal Reading

E equals h times nu.

Physicist's Reading

A physicist would read it as the energy of one quantum equals Planck's constant times the radiation frequency.

Symbol Breakdown

\(E\): the energy carried by one quantum of radiation.

\(h\): Planck's constant, the basic quantum of action.

\(\nu\): the frequency of the radiation.

Physical Meaning

This equation says energy is not exchanged continuously. It comes in discrete packets whose size is fixed by the radiation frequency.
