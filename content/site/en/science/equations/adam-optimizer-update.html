Adam Optimizer Update

Equation

$$ \theta_{t+1}=\theta_t-\eta\frac{\hat m_t}{\sqrt{\hat v_t}+\epsilon} $$

Literal Reading

theta sub open brace t plus 1 close brace equals theta sub t minus eta m sub hat sub t over open parenthesis square root open parenthesis v sub hat sub t close parenthesis plus epsilon close parenthesis.

Common Reading

Adam adapts the step size per parameter using running estimates of gradient mean and variance.

Symbols

\(\hat m_t\): bias-corrected first moment estimate of the gradient.

\(\hat v_t\): bias-corrected second moment estimate.

\(\eta\): learning rate.

\(\epsilon\): small constant preventing division by zero.

Meaning of the Equation

Adam adapts the learning step using moving averages of gradients and squared gradients.
