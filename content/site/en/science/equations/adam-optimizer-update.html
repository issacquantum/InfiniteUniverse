Adam Optimizer Update

Equation

$$ \theta_{t+1}=\theta_t-\eta\frac{\hat m_t}{\sqrt{\hat v_t}+\epsilon} $$

Literal Reading

The parameters move by a learning rate times a bias-corrected first moment divided by a scaled second moment.

Physicist's Reading

Adam adapts the step size per parameter using running estimates of gradient mean and variance.

Symbol Breakdown

\(\hat m_t\): bias-corrected first moment estimate of the gradient.

\(\hat v_t\): bias-corrected second moment estimate.

\(\eta\): learning rate.

\(\epsilon\): small constant preventing division by zero.

Physical Meaning

Adam is a practical optimization tool. It does not change what the model is learning; it changes how efficiently parameters move through the loss landscape.
