Penrose Process

Equation

$$E_\infty < 0\quad \text{inside the ergosphere}$$

Literal Reading

The energy measured at infinity can be negative inside the ergosphere.

Common Reading

Short version: The condition that allows rotational energy to be extracted from a Kerr black hole.

Symbols

\(E_\infty\): energy as measured by a distant observer.

Ergosphere: region outside the horizon where frame dragging prevents static observers.

Meaning of the Equation

The condition that allows rotational energy to be extracted from a Kerr black hole.

Scope and Limits

The inequality states the possibility of negative energy states in the ergosphere. Turning that possibility into an efficient astrophysical extraction process requires additional dynamics, not just the inequality.
