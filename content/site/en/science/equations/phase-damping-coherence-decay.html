Phase Damping Coherence Decay

Equation

$$ \rho_{01}(t) = \rho_{01}(0)\, e^{-t/T_2} $$

Literal Reading

rho sub 01 of t equals rho sub 01 of 0 e to the power of minus gamma t.

Common Reading

A simple model of dephasing: phase information fades over time even when population probabilities do not necessarily change.

Symbols

\(\rho_{01}(t)\): the off-diagonal element of the density matrix at time \(t\).

\(\rho_{01}(0)\): the initial coherence.

\(e^{-t/T_2}\): exponential decay factor.

\(T_2\): dephasing time.

Meaning of the Equation

Coherence between basis states decays without necessarily changing populations.
