Scaled Dot Product Attention

Equation

$$ \operatorname{Attention}(Q,K,V)=\operatorname{softmax}\!\left(\frac{QK^T}{\sqrt{d_k}}\right)V $$

Literal Reading

Attention of Q comma K comma V equals softmax of QK to the power of T over square root of d sub k V.

Common Reading

Attention scores compare queries with keys, normalize the scores, and use them to weight values.

Symbols

\(Q\): query matrix.

\(K\): key matrix.

\(V\): value matrix.

\(d_k\): key dimension, used to stabilize the scale of dot products.

\(\operatorname{softmax}\): converts scores into weights that sum to one.
