Equation

Common Reading

The blackbody spectral radiance is given by Planck's distribution in frequency space.

Symbols

\(B(\nu,T)\): the spectral radiance of the blackbody.

\(h\): Planck's constant.

\(\nu\): the radiation frequency.

\(c\): the speed of light.

\(k_B\): Boltzmann's constant.

\(e^{h\nu/(k_B T)}\): the exponential factor that suppresses high-frequency emission.

Meaning of the Equation

It gives the correct thermal radiation spectrum and resolves the ultraviolet catastrophe.
